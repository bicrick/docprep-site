Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 12, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to docprep. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our desktop application. Our commitment to your privacy is simple: we don't collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data.

2. Information We Collect

None. docprep is a 100% offline application. We do not collect any personal information, usage data, analytics, or telemetry of any kind.

3. How Your Data is Processed

All document processing happens entirely on your local device. When you use docprep to extract text from documents:

  • Your files never leave your computer
  • No data is sent to any server
  • No internet connection is required for the application to function
  • Extracted content is saved only to locations you specify on your device

4. Third-Party Services

docprep does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or external APIs. The application operates completely independently.

5. Data Security

Since all processing occurs locally and no data is transmitted, your documents remain as secure as your own computer. We recommend following standard security practices for your device.

6. Children's Privacy

docprep does not collect any information from anyone, including children under 13 years of age.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.

8. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you can reach out via the contact links on this website.
